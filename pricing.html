Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Members, Guests, and Viewers?

Members can create and edit content. Guests have limited edit rights on specific items they're invited to. Viewers can only view content they're invited to without making changes.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. Downgrades will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualifying nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team for more information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, and for Enterprise plans, we can also accommodate purchase orders and invoicing.
